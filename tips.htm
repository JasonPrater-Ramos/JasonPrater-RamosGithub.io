I know I just info dumped a lot of things on you so I'm just going to go ahead and give my best tips.
  • Always angle or sidecrape if you can. This can be a matter of life or death
  • Learn the weak points of tanks as you play. Instead of looking for the green indicator as this takes more
    time than just already knowing where to shoot.
  • Know your tank. Learn what it can and can't do. Learn its weak spots so you can protect them.
  • Know your teammates' tanks so you know what they can do and how you should support them.
  • Know your teammates' tanks so you know what they can do and how you should support them.
